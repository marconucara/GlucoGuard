Privacy Policy for GlucoGuard

Last updated: November 2024

Important Disclaimer: GlucoGuard is a third-party application and is NOT affiliated with, authorized, or endorsed by Abbott Laboratories. This app is for personal information purposes only and is not intended for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions.

1. Data Collection and Usage

GlucoGuard is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our own servers.

  • Credentials: Your LibreLinkUp email and password are stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences.
  • Transmission: These credentials are sent directly to Abbott's official LibreLinkUp API over a secure (HTTPS) connection to fetch your glucose readings.
  • Health Data: Glucose values are fetched in real-time to provide monitoring and alarms. This data is kept in memory for the duration of the monitoring session and is not stored persistently or uploaded elsewhere.

2. Third-Party Services

The app interacts with Abbott's LibreLinkUp API. By using this app, you are subject to Abbott's own privacy policy and terms of service regarding your health data.

3. Data Security

We use standard Android security practices to protect the data stored on your device. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device (e.g., using a PIN or password).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer via the GitHub repository where this app is hosted.
